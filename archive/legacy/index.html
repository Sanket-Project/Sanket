AI & ML Engine

A foundation model
refracted for every vertical.

PRISMA's core is a multi-modal foundation model trained on $400B+ in cross-industry transactional history, refined per-vertical through supervised fine-tuning and reinforced through continuous learning on your real-world outcomes.

⚛
Multi-Modal Foundation Model

Trained jointly on time-series, text, visual, and structured business data. The base model has cross-industry "world knowledge" of demand patterns and supply dynamics before any customer-specific training occurs.

∿
Vertical Fine-Tuning Pipeline

Each industry module ships with a pre-trained vertical head (Fashion / Electronics / Pharma). On deployment, we further fine-tune on your tenant data for SKU-, batch-, or component-level precision.

⊕
Time-Series Ensemble

A 24-model ensemble — NeuralProphet, DeepAR, N-HiTS, Temporal Fusion Transformer, plus industry-specific decomposition methods — with automatic model selection per series based on data characteristics.

∮
Causal Inference Engine

Separates genuine demand signals from promotions, anomalies, and confounders. Provides counterfactual analysis — what would have happened — so leaders can audit AI recommendations.

⇆
RL Supply Optimizer

A reinforcement-learning agent that translates forecasts into supply chain actions — reorder points, allocations, transfers — under your specific cost, lead-time, and service-level constraints.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does PRISMA tailor itself to my specific industry?
+
On onboarding, you select one or more industry modules (Apparel & Fashion, Consumer Electronics, Pharmaceuticals, with more verticals on the roadmap). Each module ships with vertical-specific data schemas, KPI definitions, dashboard templates, model architectures, and external signal feeds. The same shared AI foundation drives all verticals — but the workspace, the metrics, and the recommendations are bespoke to your industry's operational reality.
Can we operate multiple industries within one tenant?
+
Yes. Conglomerates with multiple verticals (e.g., a holding company spanning fashion and consumer electronics) can enable multiple modules under a single tenant. Each module operates in its own workspace with its own KPIs, dashboards, and access controls, but rolls up into consolidated executive reporting.
How do you handle regulated environments like pharma?
+
For pharmaceutical and life-sciences deployments, PRISMA operates in GxP-validated environments with 21 CFR Part 11-compliant audit trails, full lot/batch-level traceability, and HIPAA-eligible infrastructure. We support fully on-premises deployments for the strictest regulatory contexts. Our compliance team works directly with your QA/regulatory team during onboarding.
Is our data used to train shared models?
+
No — never, without explicit written consent. The PRISMA foundation model is trained on public, licensed, and anonymized datasets. Your tenant data is used exclusively for your own fine-tuned models. We offer federated learning options for customers who want to contribute (and benefit from) aggregate intelligence without sharing raw data.
What's the typical implementation timeline?
+
Most customers reach first live forecasts within 14 days for Growth-tier deployments and 30–60 days for Enterprise deployments involving multiple modules, custom integrations, or VPC/on-prem environments. Full ROI is typically realized within one buying cycle — usually 3–6 months depending on your operational cadence.
What if our industry isn't currently supported?
+
PRISMA's architecture is designed for vertical extensibility. We're actively building modules for CPG, automotive, and agriculture — these will ship in the next 12 months. For Enterprise customers in unsupported verticals, our solutions team can co-develop a custom module on a dedicated engagement basis.
